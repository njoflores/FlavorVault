Kimchi Scrambled Eggs

This fusion dish of kimchi scrambled eggs is a perfect way to shake up your breakfast routine. Combining the creamy texture of American scrambled eggs with the bold, spicy kick of Korean kimchi, this dish is both satisfying and quick to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • 5 eggs
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp sour cream
  • 1 tbsp kale, chopped
  • 1 tbsp kimchi (or more to taste)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs directly into a non-stick saucepan. Add butter. Do not beat the eggs or season yet. Turn the heat to medium-high.
  2. Stir constantly with a spatula, mixing the eggs and butter together.
  3. When the pan gets hot, remove it from the heat for about 20 seconds, continuing to stir. Return to heat and repeat at 30-second intervals. Cook for 2-4 minutes until the eggs reach a creamy texture.
  4. Remove from heat and immediately mix in sour cream to stop the eggs from overcooking.
  5. Add the chopped kale and kimchi. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips:

  • Serve the scrambled eggs over buttery toast for an extra indulgent breakfast.
  • Use freshly made or store-bought kimchi depending on your preference and time constraints.
  • For added texture, garnish with toasted sesame seeds or green onions.
  • Pair with a warm cup of tea or coffee for a complete meal.
