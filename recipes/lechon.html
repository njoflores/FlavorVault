Air Fryer Lechon Kawali

The air fryer makes this crispy pork belly recipe easy to make. Say goodbye to the oil splatters of traditional deep frying, and say hello to a faster, cleaner way to enjoy lechon kawali!

Ingredients:

  • 1 kilo whole pork belly (liempo)
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 cup patis (fish sauce)
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 2 pieces dried bay leaves
  • Water, as needed

Instructions:

  1. Step 1:
    Place the pork belly in a large pot. Add salt, patis, black peppercorns, and bay leaves. Pour enough water to cover the pork. Bring to a boil, then simmer until tender, about 2 hours.
  2. Step 2:
    Remove the tender pork from the water and set it aside to cool. (Reserve the water for another recipe.)
  3. Step 3:
    Once the pork has cooled, set it aside to chill overnight in the refrigerator.
  4. Step 4:
    Preheat the air fryer for 10 minutes at 200°C. Then, place the pork belly in the air fryer basket and set the timer for 10 minutes. Afterward, decrease the heat to 180°C and air fry for another 20 minutes.
