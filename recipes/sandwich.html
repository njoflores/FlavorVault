Egg and Mayo Toast

Egg and Mayo Toast is a quick and delicious dish that's popular as a snack or breakfast in Japan. With just a few ingredients and 10 minutes of cooking, this dish combines soft egg, crispy toast, and creamy Japanese mayo for a comforting treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 slice shokupan toast bread
  • Japanese mayonnaise
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Squeeze mayonnaise along the edge of the bread to create a ridge.
  2. Crack an egg into the center of the ridge and bake in a toaster oven until golden brown, about 10 minutes.
  3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper if desired.

Tips:

  • Ensure your toaster oven is preheated to ensure even cooking of the egg.
  • Feel free to add extra toppings like cheese or herbs for more flavor!
  • This dish is best enjoyed fresh, right after it’s baked for a crispy texture.
