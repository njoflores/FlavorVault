Sausage Rougail (Creole Sausage Stew)

A spicy stew of smoked sausages with fresh ginger, onions, tomatoes, and aromatics. This one-pot, easy family meal is ready in just 40 minutes!

Ingredients:

  • 4 large smoked pork sausages
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 large onions, peeled and diced
  • 6 garlic cloves, peeled and minced
  • 1.5 inch fresh ginger, peeled and grated
  • ½ bird’s eye chili, seeded and minced
  • 4 large ripe tomatoes
  • 4-5 sprigs thyme, stemmed
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt (to taste)

Instructions:

  1. Step 1:
    In a medium pot, bring water to a simmer. Poke the sausages with a fork 4 times, drop them into the pot, and simmer for 10 minutes. Remove sausages, let them cool, then dice them into ½ to ¾-inch pieces.
  2. Step 2:
    Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Add the diced onions, minced garlic, grated ginger, and minced chili. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until fragrant. Add the sausage pieces and cook for 5 more minutes.
  3. Step 3:
    Add the diced tomatoes, thyme, bay leaf, and black pepper. Reduce the heat to medium-low, cover, and cook for 20 minutes.
  4. Step 4:
    Stir and crush the tomatoes with the back of a spoon for a chunky sauce. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt if necessary. Serve with steamed rice, potatoes, lentils, or red beans.
