Strawberry Milkshake

This strawberry milkshake is creamy, frothy, and the perfect combination of fresh strawberries and vanilla ice cream. A nostalgic treat that’s ideal for hot days or when you’re craving something sweet. With a little sugar to enhance the natural sweetness of the berries, this milkshake is sure to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound fresh strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pint vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Small whole strawberries, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cut the tops off the strawberries and slice them into a few pieces.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the sliced strawberries, sugar, and vanilla extract. Stir well.
  3. Let the strawberries sit for at least 20 minutes (up to 1 hour) to macerate and release their juices.
  4. Place the strawberries (with their juices), ice cream, and milk into a blender.
  5. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  6. Pour into glasses and garnish with a whole strawberry on the rim of each glass. Serve immediately.

Tips:

  • You can use frozen strawberries for a thicker milkshake. Just add a little extra milk if needed.
  • For a strawberry-banana twist, add a banana to the blender.
  • To make a malted version, add a scoop of malt powder to the mix.
