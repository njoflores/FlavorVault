Peanut Butter Milkshake

If you're a peanut butter lover, this milkshake is a must-try. Sweet, a little salty, and incredibly creamy, this shake is both satisfying and simple to make. Enjoy it as-is, or get creative with toppings like toasted marshmallows, hot fudge, or crushed peanut butter cookies.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • Whipped cream, optional garnish
  • Chopped peanuts, optional garnish

Instructions:

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Place the ice cream, milk, and peanut butter into a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into large glasses and garnish with whipped cream and chopped peanuts.

Tips:

  • Chill your milkshake glasses in the fridge for about 15 minutes before preparing the milkshakes.
  • For smoother blending, soften the ice cream for 10-15 minutes before using.
  • Layer the ingredients in the following order: milk, ice cream, and peanut butter.
