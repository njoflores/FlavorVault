How to Make a Latte

Master the art of making a cafe-quality latte at home! With creamy milk and rich espresso, this drink is a delight for coffee lovers.

Ingredients:

  • 2 shots of espresso (2 ounces)
  • 4 ounces (1/2 cup) fresh whole milk*

Instructions:

  1. Make the espresso: Use an espresso machine or manual espresso maker to prepare two shots of espresso. Pour into a mug.
  2. Steam the milk (espresso machine): Place the milk in a steaming pitcher. Hold the steaming wand just below the surface to create foam until the milk doubles in size. Lower the wand to create silky microfoam with a wet-paint texture. Steam until the milk reaches 150°F (hot but not boiling).
  3. Froth the milk (no espresso machine): Heat milk to 150°F and froth using a milk frother, French press, or whisk. Aim for small, even bubbles and slightly thickened milk.
  4. Combine: Tap the milk container on the counter to break large bubbles. Pour steamed or frothed milk into the espresso, ending with a light foam layer on top.
  5. Serve and enjoy! Customize with your favorite flavor syrups or spices.

Tips:

  • Whole milk froths best, but oat milk is a great non-dairy alternative.
  • For perfect foam, use fresh milk close to its purchase date.
  • Experiment with frothing methods to find what works best for your equipment.
