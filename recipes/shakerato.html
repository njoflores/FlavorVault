Cocoa Caffè Shakerato

This indulgent twist on the classic Italian caffè shakerato features a creamy espresso base, enhanced with coffee liqueur, brown sugar, and a dusting of cocoa for a decadent coffee treat.

Ingredients:

  • 7 OZ. of espresso, plus ½ cup
  • 1 OZ. of coffee liqueur
  • Liquid sugar, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon peel
  • Bitter cocoa powder
  • Seeds of half a vanilla pod

Instructions:

  1. Pour ½ cup of espresso into a bowl. Mix with 2 tablespoons of brown sugar and the seeds of half a vanilla pod using an electric mixer until creamy.
  2. In a cocktail shaker, combine the remaining espresso, coffee liqueur, and 2 spoonfuls of liquid sugar. Add ice cubes and the peel of 2 lemon slices.
  3. Shake vigorously to combine and chill the mixture.
  4. Pour the contents of the shaker into serving glasses filled with ice. Sprinkle generously with cocoa powder.
  5. Top with the creamy espresso mixture and finish with another dusting of cocoa powder.

Tips:

  • For an extra kick, try adding a shot of your favorite coffee liqueur or rum.
  • Make sure to use fresh espresso for the best flavor.
