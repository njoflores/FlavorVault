Mini Salmon Pandoro Club Sandwich

These mini salmon pandoro club sandwiches combine the rich, buttery sweetness of pandoro bread with the smoothness of robiola cheese and the smokiness of salmon. A perfect appetizer or snack, ready in just 15 minutes!

Ingredients:

  • 1 LB. of pandoro (sweet yeast bread)
  • 1/3 LB. of smoked salmon
  • 1/4 LB. of robiola cheese
  • Chives
  • Lemon

Instructions:

  1. Cut the pandoro bread into horizontal slices, about 1/2 inch thick. Choose 3 slices from the middle, so that they are the most similar in size.
  2. Place them on a baking tray lined with parchment paper and toast at 340°F for 4-5 minutes.
  3. Mix the robiola cheese with a pinch of grated lemon zest and a few chives.
  4. Spread 2 of the 3 slices of bread with the cheese and top with the sliced salmon. Stack the slices and close the sandwich with the third, plain slice.
  5. Cut it into 4 wedges and serve.

Tips:

  • With the rest of the pandoro, you can make other finger sandwiches, which can also be filled with robiola and salmon, or with other assorted ingredients.
  • Alternatively, you can make an herb butter: Whip 3-5 tablespoons of butter with a whisk to make it frothy, then mix it with chives and lemon zest. Spread it on the pandoro and proceed as above.
