Homemade Hot Chocolate

A rich and creamy homemade hot chocolate recipe that’s ready in minutes! Made with staple kitchen ingredients, it’s the perfect cozy drink for the colder months.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar (or add to desired sweetness)
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 4 cups whole milk*
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate (chips, chunks, or shavings)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, whisk together the sugar and cocoa powder.
  2. Add milk, chocolate chips, and vanilla, and whisk to combine.
  3. Place over medium heat and bring to a light boil, stirring constantly until the chocolate is fully melted and incorporated.
  4. Set aside to cool to your desired serving temperature.
  5. Serve with your favorite toppings like whipped cream or marshmallows.

Tips:

  • For a richer and creamier hot chocolate, replace 1 cup of milk with 1 cup of heavy cream or half-and-half.
  • Use high-quality cocoa powder and chocolate for the best flavor.
  • Whisk continuously to prevent the cocoa and chocolate from settling or burning.
