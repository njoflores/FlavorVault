Penne with Mozzarella, Red Peppers, and Capers

Smooth, tubular penne pasta with mozzarella, roasted red peppers, and capers. This quick and easy dish is perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 1 LB. of penne pasta
  • 4 OZ. of mozzarella, diced
  • 1 roasted red bell pepper lobe, diced
  • 1 1/2 OZ. of olive oil
  • 4 small spring onions
  • Salt-packed capers, rinsed and chopped
  • Parsley, chopped
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook penne in plenty of boiling salted water until al dente.
  2. While the pasta cooks, slice the spring onions thinly and sauté with olive oil and a spoonful of capers.
  3. Add the diced roasted red pepper to the pan and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Drain the pasta once it’s al dente, then add it to the pan with the peppers and capers. Toss quickly to combine.
  5. Add the diced mozzarella. As soon as the mozzarella begins to stretch and melt, remove the pan from the heat.
  6. Sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve immediately.

Tips:

  • For extra flavor, use buffalo mozzarella for a creamier texture.
  • Try adding a pinch of red pepper flakes for a touch of heat.
