Oreo Milkshake

Oreo milkshake is a classic treat that combines creamy vanilla ice cream, milk, and crunchy Oreo cookies. Blended to perfection, this milkshake is rich, indulgent, and perfect for any sweet tooth. Serve it with extra crushed Oreos on top for that added crunch!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pint vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup milk (preferably whole milk)
  • 8 Oreo cookies (or other chocolate sandwich cookies)
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate sauce
  • 2 Oreo cookies, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place vanilla ice cream, milk, Oreo cookies, and chocolate sauce into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Crush the remaining 2 Oreo cookies for garnish by placing them in a plastic bag and crushing with a rolling pin.
  4. Pour the milkshake into tall glasses, and top with crushed Oreos. Add a straw and serve immediately!

Tips:

  • For a thicker milkshake, reduce the amount of milk or add more ice cream.
  • Use whole milk for the creamiest texture.
  • For a vegan option, replace the ice cream with non-dairy ice cream and use plant-based milk.
