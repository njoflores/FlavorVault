Genmaicha Latte

Genmaicha Latte is a warm and comforting drink made from Genmaicha, a Japanese green tea blended with roasted brown rice. This latte combines the toasty, nutty aroma of Genmaicha with rich, frothy milk, making it a perfect treat for the colder seasons. With less caffeine than regular green tea, it's an ideal choice for those looking for a milder, soothing beverage. Enjoy it as a cozy afternoon indulgence or a relaxing evening drink.

Ingredients:

  • 2/3 cup hot water
  • 2 Tbsp Genmaicha green tea
  • 1 Tbsp sugar (or other sweetener)
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Steep the Genmaicha tea in very hot water for a couple of minutes.
  2. Add sugar or your preferred sweetener to the tea and stir to combine.
  3. Froth the milk using a milk frother or by heating and whisking until frothy.
  4. Pour the frothed milk over the brewed tea and serve warm.

Tips:

  • Adjust the strength of the tea by adding more or less Genmaicha depending on your taste.
  • For a creamier texture, use whole milk or a milk alternative with a high-fat content, such as oat milk.
  • If you prefer a sweeter drink, add more sugar or try a flavored syrup like vanilla.
