Mai Tai

The Mai Tai is a classic Tiki cocktail, beloved for its refreshing blend of rum, orange curaçao, lime juice, and orgeat. A perfect showcase for quality rum, this timeless drink is ideal for tropical vibes and cocktail enthusiasts alike.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 ounces white rum
  • 3/4 ounce orange curaçao
  • 3/4 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1/2 ounce orgeat
  • 1/2 ounce dark rum
  • Garnish: lime wheel
  • Garnish: mint sprig

Instructions:

  1. Add the white rum, curaçao, lime juice, and orgeat into a shaker with crushed ice. Shake lightly for about 3 seconds.
  2. Pour the mixture into a double rocks glass.
  3. Float the dark rum over the top of the drink.
  4. Garnish with a lime wheel and mint sprig. Serve immediately.

Tips:

  • Use high-quality rum for a well-balanced and flavorful cocktail.
  • Crushed ice enhances the drink's texture and presentation.
  • Experiment with different rum blends to create your own unique version of the Mai Tai.
