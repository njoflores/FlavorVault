Ube Champorado

Ube Champorado is a twist on the classic Filipino chocolate rice porridge. Instead of the usual chocolate, this version uses the earthy, sweet flavor of ube to create a unique breakfast treat that’s sure to delight.

Ingredients:

  • 1 250-gram cup malagkit rice, rinsed
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cup ube halaya
  • 1 teaspoon ube flavoring
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream (plus more to serve)
  • Evaporated milk, to taste
  • Condensed milk, to taste
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Step 1:
    Pour rice and water into a medium stockpot over medium heat. Bring to a boil, then lower to a simmer until the rice is cooked. Stir regularly to avoid scorching and sticking. Add more water as necessary to achieve your desired consistency.
  2. Step 2:
    Stir in ube halaya, ube flavoring, coconut cream, and a pinch of salt to taste. Cook, stirring, until heated through and the ube is well-mixed.
  3. Step 3:
    Ladle champorado into bowls and top with a swirl of evaporated milk or condensed milk to taste.
