Margarita

The Margarita is a classic tequila-based cocktail that balances the tang of fresh lime juice, the sweetness of orange liqueur, and the bold flavors of tequila. It's an iconic drink that remains a favorite for its simplicity and refreshing profile.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces blanco tequila
  • 1/2 ounce orange liqueur
  • 1 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1/2 ounce agave syrup
  • Garnish: lime wheel
  • Garnish: kosher salt (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice and add the tequila, orange liqueur, lime juice, and agave syrup.
  2. Shake well until the mixture is chilled.
  3. Strain into a rocks glass filled with fresh ice.
  4. Garnish with a lime wheel and a salted rim, if desired.

Tips:

  • Use 100% blue agave tequila for the best flavor.
  • Freshly squeezed lime juice is essential for a superior Margarita.
  • To rim the glass with salt, run a lime wedge along the edge and dip it into kosher salt.
  • Adjust the sweetness by adding more or less agave syrup to taste.
