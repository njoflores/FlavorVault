Burong Mangga (Pickled Green Mango)

Burong Mangga is a simple and easy-to-make pickled green mango dish, perfect as a side or snack. The sour-sweet flavor of the mangoes is intensified during the pickling process, creating a delightful treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large green mango
  • 1 liter water, boiled and cooled
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt

Instructions:

  1. Step 1:
    Peel the green mango and cut off the mango cheeks. Slice the cheeks into strips.
  2. Step 2:
    Soak the sliced mangoes overnight in the boiled and cooled water.
  3. Step 3:
    Make the syrup: In a saucepan over medium heat, mix water, sugar, and salt, then bring to a boil. Let the syrup cool completely.
  4. Step 4:
    Drain the mangoes and discard the water. Place the mango slices in an airtight container.
  5. Step 5:
    Pour the syrup over the mangoes, filling the container as full as possible. Refrigerate overnight or longer before eating.
