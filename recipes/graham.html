Fruit Cocktail Graham Float

Fruit Cocktail Graham Float is a no-bake dessert made with layers of graham crackers, whipped cream, and fruit cocktail. It’s easy to prepare and perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients:

  • 2 packs (250 ml each) all-purpose cream, very cold
  • 1 can (14 ounces) condensed milk, very cold
  • 18 pieces graham crackers
  • 1 can (30 ounces) fruit cocktail, drained well

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine all-purpose cream and condensed milk. Using a hand mixer at low speed, beat mixture until it begins to thicken. Increase speed to medium and continue to beat for about 8 to 10 minutes or until double in volume.
  2. Arrange one layer of graham crackers on the bottom of the pan, breaking pieces as needed to fit sides.
  3. Spoon ⅓ of the cream mixture on graham crackers and spread to cover.
  4. Arrange fruit cocktail on top of the cream.
  5. Arrange another layer of graham crackers over the fruit.
  6. Spoon and spread ⅓ of the cream mixture. Add another layer of fruit.
  7. Arrange the last layer of graham crackers on top of the fruit. Spoon and spread remaining ⅓ of the cream mixture on top of crackers. Arrange the remaining fruit cocktail on top of the cream.
  8. Cover tightly with plastic film and refrigerate overnight. Freeze for about 1 hour before serving, if you like.
  9. To serve, cut into squares.

Pro Tips:

  • For maximum volume, chill the cream and condensed milk until very cold. You can also chill the bowl and the beaters in the freezer for about 20 to 30 minutes.
  • Drain the canned fruit well to keep the extra liquid from seeping into the cake.
  • Cover tightly with plastic wrap, pressing the film on the surface of the top cake layer to prevent ice crystals.

Serving Suggestions and Storage:

  • Serve chilled as a refreshing dessert or snack.
  • Best enjoyed immediately, but it can be stored in the freezer for up to a week.
